About Us

Flensburger Beers are a well-known German brand, famous for their traditional brewing methods and high-quality ingredients. The brewery, Flensburger Brauerei, was founded in 1888 and is located in Flensburg, near the Danish border.

One of the unique characteristics of Flensburger beers is their use of pure spring water from the underground glacier springs of Northern Germany, which gives their beers a distinct, fresh taste.

Brews Talk. Stomachs Growl.

Flensburger Beer, commonly known as "Flens", is a popular German beer brand that has been brewed in Flensburg, in northern Germany, since 1888. It's famous for its distinctive swing-top bottles and the unique "Plop!" sound when the bottle is opened. This sound has become part of the beer's identity and is featured in many of its advertisements.
